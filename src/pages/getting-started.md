# Getting started

Welcome to the Setics Sttar documentation. Setics Sttar is a suite of
fiber-optic network design and optimization products. This page is the common
entry point — it is open to any signed-in user and is **not** gated by product
license type.

## Pick your product

| Product | License | What it does | Manual |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Advanced Designer** | `STAD` | Full interactive desktop design & optimization. | [Open](/advanced-designer/intro) |
| **Planner** | `STPL` | Desktop, restricted operator profile. | [Open](/planner/intro) |
| **Batch Engine** | `STBE` | Command-line / server batch runs. | [Open](/batch-engine/intro) |
| **Cloud** | *TBD* | Hosted service (same engine, in the browser). | [Open](/cloud/intro) |

## Four steps to your first design

1. **Check the requirements.** Confirm your workstation (or license server)
   meets the [System requirements](/system-requirements).
2. **Download and install.** Get the installer for your version from the
   [Downloads](/downloads) page and run it; the setup assistant installs the
   prerequisites (.NET, Visual C++, CodeMeter) if they are missing.
3. **Set up your license.** Setics Sttar uses the **CodeMeter** licensing
   system — see [Licensing](/licensing) for workstation vs. network licenses and
   the CodeMeter runtime.
4. **Start designing.** Launch the product and follow its getting-started flow:
   Advanced Designer's [Quick launch](/advanced-designer/quick-launch), the
   Planner walkthrough, or the Batch Engine [CLI guide](/batch-engine/intro).

:::tip Where to go next
Each manual's sidebar follows the same flow as step 4 in full detail —
prepare data → configure → optimize → validate → report and export.
:::
