# Technical Support Contact Details

There is a single contact email for any support request:
**[support.sttar@setics.com](mailto:support.sttar@setics.com)**.

You can also connect to your online support centre at
**[support.setics-sttar.com](https://support.setics-sttar.com)** to report bugs,
suggest changes, or download resources for Setics Sttar users — sign in with the
username and password provided with your Setics Sttar license.

:::info
If you don't yet have your login details, request them from your sales
representative or email [support.sttar@setics.com](mailto:support.sttar@setics.com).
:::
